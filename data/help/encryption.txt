Encryption Help

Encryption Help:

To encrypt the currently opened file, select Encryption from the Encryption menu
and input a password.

To decrypt the currently opened file, select Decryption from the Encryption menu 
and input the same password that you used when encrypting the file.

Passwords must be 6 or more characters long.

Make sure to save changes after encrypting or decrypting a file.

Do not modify an encrypted file, or you may be unable to decrypt it afterwards.
